Lasagna Recipe

How to make lasagna

Lasagna, when made right, is one of the most delicious dishes in the world. By following the below instructions, you will be making the lasagna of other peoples dreams!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: One onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products:One can crushed tomatoes, two cans tomato sauce, two cans tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tbs white sugar
  • Spices and seasoning: Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: Store bought or freshly made
  • Cheeses: Parmasean, Mozzarella and ricotta cheeses
  • Egg: Two eggs
