Tater tot casserole Recipe

How to make tater tot casserole

Tater tot casserole is something that is simple to make and delicious to eat. By following the below instructions, you will be a master of making a tater tot casserole!

Ingredients

  • Beef: Ground beef cooked up and crumbly.
  • Canned soup: One can of condensed cream of mushroom soup.
  • Seasonings:Salt and black pepper.
  • Tater tots: One bag frozen tater tots.
  • Cheese: Shredded cheddar cheese.

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef until brown. Add soup seasoning and stir.
  2. Transfer the beef to a baking dish. Top with tater tots then add cheese.
  3. Bake until tater tots are golden brown.
  4. Remove from oven and let the tater tots rest before serving
