Chicken Parm Recipe

How to make chicken parm

Chicken parm is one of the easiest and delicious dishes to make in the world. By following the below instructions, you will be making a chicken parm that is the talk of the town!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray: Olive oil
  • Chicken: 16 ounce package of boneless chicken breasts
  • Cheese:4 slices of mopzzarella cheese
  • Tomatoes: 2 cups of tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Spray frying pan with cooking oil. Place chicken on the pan.
  2. Bake for 20 minutes, flipping the chicken after 12 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken from the oven and add desired amount of tomato sauce to tops of chicken.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top of each piece of chicken.
  5. Place pan back in oven and bake until cheese is melted and the juices run clear.
  6. Remove from oven and let rest. Thermometer should read 165 degrees.
